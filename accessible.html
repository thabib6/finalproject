Why Accessibility Is Important?

  • It allows for you and every single user to get a better experience in your website.
  • Certain users such as users with disabilities will have a difficult time with some of the content so making it accessible for them would allow them to participate and enchance the website.
  • It promotes equality to everyone and promotes usability.
