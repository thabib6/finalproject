Ethical Issues In Web Development:

  • Privacy and Security from collecting users data.
  • Accessibility and Inclusivness for users to be able to participate.
  • Being Accountable and Responsible with the information gained and provided through the website.

Legal Issues In Web Development:

  • Copyrighting other users content without permission or stating the owner.
  • Cybersecurity issues such as hacking that can lead to data being stolen or lost.
