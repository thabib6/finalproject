Semantic Elements I Use:

  • header - Its the top part of the website where you do an intro and where I put my title.
  • nav - Navigation links in a block that allows users to find their resources.
  • main - This where you insert the main content of the image.
  • section - The section element allows there to be groups for individual content.
  • article - The article element is used to enclosed self-contained content.
  • details - Hides or shows additional content.
  • summary - It is like a heading for the details element.
  • time - Used to show the date or a specific period of time.
  • footer - It is at the bottom part of the website that is used as the concluding part of the page.
